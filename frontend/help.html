Help & FAQ

How do I combine files quickly?

Select Quick Upload, drag your files (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, images), and hit Combine. You’ll instantly get a single merged file.

What is Checklist Mode?

Checklist Mode allows you to create sections (like “Exam Files”, “Receipts”, etc.) and upload files under each. The final PDF will have divider pages for each section so you never lose track.

What file types are supported?

We support: PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, JPG, PNG, GIF. Other formats may be added later.

Are my files safe?

Yes ✅. Files are processed temporarily and deleted automatically after the combined output is generated. We don’t store or share your files.

Troubleshooting

  • “Upload failed” → Check file type or size.
  • “Combination failed” → Try re-uploading smaller sets.
  • If problem persists, contact support below.
