Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Data Handling

We process the files you upload (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, JPG, PNG, etc.) solely for the purpose of combining them into a single output file. Files are not stored permanently and are deleted automatically from our server after processing.

2. File Storage

Your uploaded files exist temporarily in a secure environment during the merging process. Once the final output is generated and downloaded, temporary files are removed. We do not keep copies, backups, or archives of your data.

3. Security

We use standard security practices to handle files safely. However, please note that no system is 100% secure. By using our service, you acknowledge that you upload and combine files at your own risk.

4. Usage Limitations

You agree not to upload harmful, malicious, or illegal content. We reserve the right to block or restrict usage if misuse is detected.

5. Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., how many times the service was used, file types combined) for improving the service. We do not log or track the actual content of your files.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your files to any third parties.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@filecombinerpro.com.
